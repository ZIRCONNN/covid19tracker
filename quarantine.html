Quarantine Guidelines

Stay at Home

If you have been exposed to COVID-19 or experiencing symptoms, it is important to stay at home and self-quarantine. Avoid contact with others to prevent the spread of the virus.

Monitor Your Symptoms

Keep track of your symptoms and seek medical attention if they worsen. Common symptoms include fever, cough, and difficulty breathing.

Stay Connected

Stay connected with your loved ones through phone calls, video chats, or social media. It's important to maintain social connections even while in quarantine.
